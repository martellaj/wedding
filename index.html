OUR LOVELINE

There's been many important events in our life together. Here are a few we'll never forget.

October 21, 2013

The first message

Both being new to Seattle, we met on OKCupid in the fall of 2013. Joe reached out with a simple question about How I Met Your Mother.

October 24, 2013

Our first date

Our first date was at a trendy bar in Belltown called List. Drink what we drank during our cocktail hour at the reception.

November 12, 2013

We started dating

After a whirlwhind of dates during our first month together, we decided that what we had was something real and we were going to commit to each other.

March 1, 2014

Our first trip together

We took our first trip together to San Francisco with a few friends. Kind of crazy... but we booked those tickets 4 months in advance after only knowing each other for barely 2 months.

June 15, 2014

Our first home together

We (officially) moved in together. This picture was taken at our fratty housewarming party. Mo's beer taste has improved since then.

September 13, 2014

Our first baby together

We decided to bring this little girl into our lives. Shannon felt outnumbered by the guys in the family.

May 16, 2015

The proposal

After about a year and a half together was all it took. We knew we were ready to take the next step. We bought a ring, Shannon annoyed Joe about it for a few weeks, and then he proposed. She said yes.
